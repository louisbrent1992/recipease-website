# Raw App Screenshots

Please upload clean, **unedited** screenshots of your app's UI to this directory. 

Do not include stylized backgrounds, marketing text over the image, or device frames. These files will be used by the Marketing AI Agent as pristine "base images" when generating new, localized, and styled marketing screenshots for Apple App Store and Google Play listing updates!

### Folder Organization & Device Sizes
To ensure the agent generates the correctly sized imagery for the storefronts, please organize your raw screenshots into device-specific subdirectories matching the target platform/screen. 

Example structure:
- \`/raw/iphone_65/\` (for 6.5" iPhone XS Max / Pro Max sizes)
- \`/raw/iphone_55/\` (for 5.5" iPhone Plus sizes)
- \`/raw/ipad_129/\` (for 12.9" iPad Pro)
- \`/raw/android_phone/\` (for standard Google Play phone sizes)
- \`/raw/android_tablet/\` (for standard 7" or 10" Android tablets)

### Supported Formats
- \`.png\`
- \`.jpg\`
- \`.jpeg\`
